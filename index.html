Asymptotic Notations

1.Big-Oh Notation:

↓

Here, g(n) is greater than or equal to f(n)

i.e; g(n) >= c*f(n)

2.Big-Omega Notation:

↓

Here, g(n) is less than or equal to f(n)

i.e; g(n) <= c*f(n)

3.Theta Notation:

↓

Here, g(n) is nearly equal to the f(n)

i.e; c1*f(n) <= g(n) <=c2*f(n)

4.Little-Oh Notation:

↓

Here, g(n) is strictly greater than f(n)

i.e; g(n) > c*f(n)

5.Little-Omega Notation:

↓

Here, g(n) is strictly less than f(n)

i.e; g(n) < c*f(n)
